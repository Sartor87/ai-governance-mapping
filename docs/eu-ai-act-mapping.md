# EU AI Act → unified controls

**Source:** [Regulation (EU) 2024/1689](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/eli/reg/2024/1689/oj)
(the AI Act, in force Aug 2024; high-risk obligations phasing in through 2026–2027).
The controls below target the **high-risk system** obligations in Chapter III,
Section 2. Classify your system first — not every system is high-risk.

Mappings are interpretive aids, not legal advice.
