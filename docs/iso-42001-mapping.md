# ISO/IEC 42001:2023 → unified controls

**Source:** [ISO/IEC 42001:2023](https://www.iso.org/standard/42001) — the AI
management system (AIMS) standard. Like other ISO management-system standards it
pairs clause requirements (4–10) with an **Annex A** set of controls; Annex B
gives implementation guidance.

Mappings are interpretive aids, not a certification claim.

ISO/IEC 42001 is the umbrella process standard; the NIST AI RMF supplies the
risk method and the EU AI Act supplies the legal obligations that the AIMS
operationalizes.
